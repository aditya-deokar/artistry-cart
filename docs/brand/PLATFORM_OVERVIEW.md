# Platform Overview

## Executive Summary

**Artistry Cart** is a premium creative marketplace that bridges the gap between artistic vision and handcrafted reality. Unlike traditional ecommerce platforms, we empower buyers to **visualize their ideas through AI-generated concepts** and connect with skilled artisans who can bring those visions to life.

---

## Target Audience

### Buyers

| Segment | Description | Primary Motivation |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Art Collectors** | Passionate about unique, one-of-a-kind pieces | Exclusivity, investment |
| **Interior Designers** | Sourcing for client projects | Quality, customization |
| **Gift Seekers** | Looking for meaningful, personalized gifts | Uniqueness, story |
| **Creative Professionals** | Need custom materials for projects | Collaboration, quality |
| **Home Decorators** | Curating personal living spaces | Aesthetic, authenticity |

### Sellers (Artisans)

| Segment | Description | Primary Need |
|---------|-------------|--------------|

## Competitive Positioning

### Market Landscape

| Platform | Strengths | Weaknesses | Our Differentiation |
|----------|-----------|------------|---------------------|
| **Etsy** | Large marketplace, established trust | Overcrowded, quality inconsistency | Curated premium focus |
| **Fiverr** | Service-based, custom work | Not product-focused, transactional | Artisan-centric, relationship-driven |
| **Artsi** | AI art focus | Limited to digital art | Physical + digital, AI as ideation tool |
| **WorkOf** | High-end design | Limited categories | Broader artisan categories |
| **Society6** | Print-on-demand | Mass production feel | Genuine handcrafted products |

### Key Differentiators

1. **AI Vision Studio** - No other platform offers AI-powered concept generation for custom orders
2. **Premium Curation** - Quality over quantity, verified artisans
3. **Collaboration Tools** - Real-time chat, milestone tracking, progress updates
4. **Artist-First** - Better commission rates, growth resources, community
